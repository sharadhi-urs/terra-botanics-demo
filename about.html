Our Story

From Hamburg, with love

Born from Nature, Backed by Science

Terra Botanics was founded in 2020 in Hamburg, Germany with a simple mission: create skincare that's genuinely good for your skin and the planet.

Every product is formulated using sustainably sourced botanicals, cold-pressed oils, and plant-based actives. No parabens, no sulfates, no synthetic fragrances. Just pure, effective ingredients.

We work directly with organic farms across Europe to source our raw ingredients, ensuring fair trade practices and minimal environmental impact at every step.
